Career Aspirations

I am currently studying product design A-level which, out of my three options, is the subject I want to continue into higher education. Throughout my studies I have come to realise that designing and creating a product that could help someone else is what really excites me. The work experience I did, which I have shown on slide 12 and 13, let me help to fulfil the design needs of clients, seeing their joy when the design was complete has encouraged my passion in product design.

Experience & Growth

Through both my A-level course and my work experience, I have become more aware of the details involved in product design. The appearance and utility of a product is now something I am constantly aware of – noting examples of both good design and those which could be improved!

I am really looking forward to continuing my studies in this field and hopefully pursuing a career in product design.
