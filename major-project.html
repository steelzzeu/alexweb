Collapsible Bike Stand

Major Project Product Development Download Specifications

Project Overview

My major project idea was to create something that gave cyclists a place where they could fix their bike. Through development of sketches and mind maps I started to figure out a way I could make this. I decided to design a bike stand which could collapse in on itself so the user could take it anywhere they wanted.
